What happens after a request?

  1. The customer submits moving details and sees an estimate immediately.
  2. You receive the request summary and review the closest suitable driver.
  3. You connect the driver and customer yourself, keeping the service personal.
Why it works

Designed for the real messiness of residential moving.

Residential moving jobs are usually small, urgent, and detail-heavy. The site focuses on the information people actually need to share to make the match work.

For customers

Less back-and-forth

They can request a move, choose vehicle size, and describe access issues without waiting for a call first.

For drivers

Better-fit jobs

Drivers only get connected to moves that match their vehicle size, service areas, and availability profile.

For your business

Control stays with you

You remain the trusted dispatcher, which protects service quality and keeps relationships in your hands.

How the product is split

Each page now has a clearer job to do.

The website is structured around the three people in the loop: the customer, the driver, and the operator.

Customer page

Book a move

Customers enter pickup and drop-off locations, select truck size, and see the estimate before submitting.

 Driver page

Register drivers

Capture truck details, contact information, helpers, and service areas so dispatch gets easier over time.

 Operator page

Manage dispatch

Monitor incoming requests, compare available drivers, and send the handoff from one organized screen.
