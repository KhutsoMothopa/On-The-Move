What happens after a request?

  1. The customer submits moving details and sees an estimate immediately.
  2. You receive the request summary and decide how to source the right bakkie or truck.
  3. You connect the customer with the move solution yourself, keeping the service personal.
Why it works

Designed for the real messiness of residential moving.

Residential moving jobs are usually small, urgent, and detail-heavy. The site focuses on the information people actually need to share to make the match work.

For customers

For operations
