How it works

Built for residential and apartment moves, not industrial trucking.

01

Customer requests a driver

They enter pickup and drop-off addresses, truck size, move date, and any helpers they need.

02

The platform estimates the cost

Pricing is calculated from the selected vehicle size, route distance, and extra move requirements.

03

You dispatch the nearest driver

Driver registrations stay in the local database so the dispatch board can suggest the closest match.
