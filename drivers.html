Driver onboarding

Build a driver network that is actually usable at dispatch time.

Good driver records are more than names and phone numbers. You want vehicle size, service areas, helpers, and starting location so you can spot the best match quickly.

Vehicle fit Size aware

A strong profile includes

  • Truck or bakkie size
  • Base suburb and service areas
  • Helper availability and vehicle registration
UX goal

Fewer blank or weak records

The form now guides the operator through what matters most so the database stays useful later.

Why these details matter

  • Truck size helps avoid mismatched jobs and wasted trips.
  • Base location makes it easier to identify who is closest to pickup.
  • Service areas show where the driver is realistically willing to work.
  • Helpers and notes make the dispatch decision faster when the job is urgent.
Best practice

Capture a few high-quality driver profiles first. A smaller but clearer database is better than many incomplete records.
